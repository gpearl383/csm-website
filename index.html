IT Spend Optimization

Most organizations are overpaying for technology by 20–40% without knowing it. We find the waste, renegotiate the contracts, right-size the licenses, and put that budget back to work — without cutting capability or reliability.

What we do

Where we find the savings

A structured IT spend audit typically uncovers significant recoverable budget within the first 30 days.

IT Spend Audit

A comprehensive review of all technology contracts, subscriptions, SaaS licenses, and vendor relationships — identifying redundancies, unused seats, and immediate negotiation opportunities.

Vendor Consolidation

Rationalize your vendor landscape, eliminate redundant tools, and consolidate contracts to reduce administrative overhead and improve negotiating leverage across your entire technology stack.

Contract Renegotiation

We benchmark your current contracts against market rates and negotiate on your behalf — typically recovering 15–30% on major software and infrastructure agreements at renewal time.

Cloud Cost Optimization

Azure and AWS spend analysis, resource right-sizing, reserved instance planning, and waste elimination. Most organizations reduce cloud spend by 20–35% without any loss of performance.

License Right-Sizing

Microsoft 365, Salesforce, Adobe, and other SaaS platform audits — ensuring you're not paying for unused seats, duplicate users, or premium tiers your team doesn't actually use.

Ongoing Governance

Monthly IT spend governance as part of a Fractional CIO engagement — so cost creep doesn't return after the initial optimization and you stay ahead of renewal cycles.
